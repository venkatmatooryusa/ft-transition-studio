Step 2 · Catalyst Conversations

Triangulate your Ethical Tug through injustice, natural compulsion, and chosen contribution.

Lens 1

The Ethical Tug

Out of all the injustices and inefficiencies in the world, which one makes you feel a genuine, persistent ethical tug — so strong that ignoring it would feel like a failure of your own character?

Lens 2

The Unique Compulsion

What specific aspect of that injustice do you personally feel compelled to correct, even if you weren’t paid? What unpaid, persistent effort do you already make—do you naturally clarify, sort, protect, connect, or build bridges?

Clarify complexity
Connect people
Protect the vulnerable
Design better systems

Lens 3

The Contribution

How do you uniquely want to contribute to solving this? Which capability do you want to weaponize—what do you want to become exceptionally strong at, in service of this injustice?

This will combine all three lenses into a draft Ethical Tug statement you can refine and save.
